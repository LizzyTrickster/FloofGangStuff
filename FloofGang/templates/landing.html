OwO What's this?

This is a small thing that I ( LiizziiVR / Lizzian ) whipped up that lets members of the Floof Gang Discord guild note their birthdays down so that TheVirtualFloof can wish them a happy birthday during his streams.
Head on over to the submission page to submit yours or visit Today's Birthdays to see a list of other users who have their birthday today!

I have some concerns...

The only data I store is your Discord/Twitch UserID (for anti-spam measures) and the day & month of your birthday.
I don't store the year (in fact, you can't even enter it) or any other details from you. I don't want to and don't need to know them.

I've found a potential security hole!

Ping me (Liizzii#0001 / LiizziiVR or one of the other mods to notify me) on discord or send me an email to lizzy-AT-lizzian.uk.
Alternatively if you're savvy with Python and Django, poke through the code and open a PR at the code repository for this site so I can also learn how to not make the same mistake in the future :)
